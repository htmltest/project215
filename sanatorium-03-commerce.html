Service standards

The customer journey from booking to checkout must follow a set of rules. From the speed of processing the application and food to passing the procedure and collecting feedback. This is the only way to achieve loyalty from customers who expect to receive a certain level of service with each visit.
Standards control is the most important function of automation, gives customers equal rights and eliminates human errors. In the Sanatorium, the client’s path is digitized and understandable to all participants, deviations from the standards and negativity are recorded and included in the report.

Mobile application for patients

Treatment program control panel. A new sales channel with a description of additional services and the possibility of self-registration. The place where the results of examinations and medical recommendations are stored. A “smart” schedule that will tell you the rules for the procedure (on an empty stomach, a preliminary shower), where to look for an office, what to take with you, and remind you of the appointed time in advance.

Guest personal account and loyalty programs

Motivate clients to come back and reward the most profitable clients with points, procedures as a gift, discounts on accommodation in a sanatorium. In your personal account, you can track the progress and status of the client in the loyalty program, purchase history and how much is left until the next step.

Credit and deposit zone

Make additional services more accessible by eliminating the need to pay for them at the checkout every time.
The ability to pay from a deposit or upon departure increases revenue from paid services by 17-20%.

Dynamic prices

The best alternative to the price chart. Manage room and office occupancy based on real demand: earn more during high season and keep working during low season. Automatic testing of elasticity of demand will help you find the best price, taking into account the cost price for any period of time.

Focus on target customers

In Sanatorium reports, you will see which clients bring you more money and you can target ads specifically to them. Geography, gender, age, marital status, preferences and interests, nosology. Segment the most priority groups, create an offer for them, record the growth of conversion and revenue.

Single booking portal

Shared booking management ecosystem for in-house salespeople and agents. Personal settings for quotas and tariffs. Instant check of available rooms in a single database, booking / cancellation in 30 seconds. Real-time updates of rates and special offers, dynamic prices and booking analytics.

Room defragmentation

Booking not by individual rooms, but by categories, allows the system to minimize the “gaps” in the loading of rooms and increase the capacity of the room fund by 5-12%. With automatic defragmentation, you can sell more room-days without additional investment in infrastructure.

Tariff inheritance

For complex tariff scales (taking into account the duration of treatment, procedures included in the program, client status), use the “tariff inheritance” function, when changes to pricing policy elements automatically change all related tariffs.
