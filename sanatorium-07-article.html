Electronic medical records (IB)

The main document in which everything that happens with the patient is recorded. The basic fields of the medical history (name, age, place of residence) enter the system at the placement stage. Each IB is created on the basis of electronic survey forms that are filled in by medical staff. The results of visits to each doctor of the sanatorium, the results of analyzes, studies, and procedures are loaded into the history. When the patient arrives again, information about him will be easy to find.
