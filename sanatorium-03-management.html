Management

Report constructor. Protection against staff abuse.
Full compliance with local legislation. Sanatorium is a tool for those who make management decisions based on up-to-date data.
Sign up for a demo

More than 300 ready-made reports on the work of the sanatorium

All figures for the manager in one window: track commercial performance, staff efficiency, customer satisfaction. The digitization of each process makes it possible to conduct experiments and evaluate the results of the introduction of new practices, the popularity of new programs and services.

Single booking portal

General environment for managing reservations, to which external agents can be connected.

Schedule for patients and staff

Automatic distribution of procedures according to the length of stay of patients. Uniform loading of medical personnel.

3D monitoring center

The key results of the sanatorium’s work in a visual form — on a PC or tablet: 3D visualization of all buildings, data on the occupancy of rooms and offices, revenue and profit indicators for each program. The monitoring center is your captain’s bridge, where operational information is collected and all problem areas are highlighted.

Employee motivation based on indicators

Make HR decisions based on facts. The system tracks key performance indicators (number of admitted patients, repeat rate, reviews) and helps to identify the most valuable employees.

Protection against official abuse and waste

The system protects the interests of the sanatorium and makes it impossible to leave procedures and extend the stay “past the cash desk”. Automated protocols of check-in, reception, release of services, the “night audit” function excludes possible abuse of personnel.

Training and support

During the implementation, our team conducts full-time training for all users and system administrators and, if necessary, sets up working interfaces for each specialist. After implementation, we leave training videos and instructions. Technical support staff will promptly solve any technical problem and help to adapt new employees of the sanatorium.

System updates

Every 3-4 months the system is supplemented with new functions and adapted to changes in legislation and the requirements of regulatory authorities. Updates are free for all Sanatorium clients.

Ready integration with related IT systems

The system has more than 80 integrations with external devices and IT systems — from electronic locks to CRM and restaurant systems. “Sanatorium” is easily integrated into the information environment of the resort
