Accommodation

Our solution combines international hospitality standards with the needs of wellness facilities: it is deeply integrated with the medical unit, allows you to simultaneously work with rooms and beds, keep track of days and nights.
Sign up for a demo

Single booking portal

The maximum speed of creating a reservation and convenient tracking of the number of rooms. General environment for the work of own managers and external partners, with the ability to regulate quotas, tariffs, overbooking.

Performance metrics analytics

Report constructor with up-to-date data on occupancy, revenue per room, profitability of all categories of rooms — both for one resort and for network facilities. ADR, RevPac, RevPar, guests, services, income — everything you need to make decisions.

Room stock defragmentation

Increasing the turnover of the room fund by 5-12% due to booking not by rooms, but by their categories. The system automatically closes “gaps” in the room occupancy schedule and allows you to accommodate more customers without investing in the construction of new rooms.

Dynamic prices

Managing room occupancy based on real demand: earn more during high season and keep working during low season. Automatic testing of elasticity of demand will help you find the best price, taking into account the cost price for any period of time.

Night Audit

The procedure for fixing the results of the day: the state of accounts, all departures / arrivals. The function protects against malfeasance, since after the audit it is impossible to make changes retroactively.

Self check-in

Contactless registration of guests — from home or through the terminal in the lobby of the resort. Arriving people scan documents and receive a key card from the room. When registering, you can explore the territory of the sanatorium on a 3D map, the location of the buildings, select a room (upgrade is available), order additional options (access to the gym, excursions, pillow menu, etc.).

Interface with fiscal registrar

Allows you to connect the room management system of the resort with a registrar for fiscalizing the amounts received from the guest and spent in the system.

Maid module

Send a cleaning plan to your maid’s mobile/tablet, keep track of room cleaning status, and send a supervisor for spot checks. Supervisors can rate cleanliness in the mobile app, and you can reward maids with the best results.

Electronic door locks

The room key (RFID cards, bracelets) is issued based on the reservation data and will only work during the period specified in the room management system. The possibility of issuing a key bypassing the system is practically excluded.
