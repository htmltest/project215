Guest journey map

Automation of the sanatorium takes place in several stages.
Each of them is designed to reduce costs and make the process as comfortable as possible for the Customer.
Order a consultation
High service is where the time of customers is valued and the best results are achieved through personalization. To achieve quality care, ensure fast, synchronized and convenient communication between medical staff.
How to collect and process feedback

Automation of medical processes allows you to meet new customer values and needs. Fast results, accurate prescriptions, convenient control and reliable data storage. In the article we will talk about a tool that raises the quality and efficiency of service in the health resort to a new level.

Automation from booking to reporting
Booking
The client purchased a ticket with the Healthy Heart treatment program. It includes a set of requirements and procedures.
Marketer
The marketer sees an increase in demand for the “Healthy Heart” program and redirects the advertising budget to other products of the sanatorium
SMS / E-mail
Notifications about successful booking and payment of the trip.
Self Check-in
Self-registration from home: choosing a room for 3D visualization, acquaintance with the territory and location of buildings. Paid options for additional profit: transfer, upgrade, additional medical services, access to the SPA area (pool/sauna), gym, meals, equipment rental (bicycles, roller skates), maid arrival time, pillow menu, guided tours.
Chief medical officer
The chief physician evaluates the future workload of offices and specialists involved in treatment programs
Chief of the sanatorium
The director sees the current forecast for room loading, revenue and profit
0 / 0
Settlement
The client arrives at the sanatorium, receives a key card from his room. Downloads a mobile app for guests.
Placement Service
An employee of the placement service creates a new medical history and gives the client automatically completed documents for signature: a contract, informed consent for the provision of medical services.
RFID card (bracelet)
The card serves as a guest ID. This is not only a room key, but also a pass for the procedure, and a means of payment linked to the client account.
Attending physician
The doctor receives a notification that he has a new patient, and can prepare for the first appointment, study the medical history (if the patient came to the sanatorium again or was in another sanatorium of the network).
Patient's Mobile app
Automated communication with the client and the opportunity to learn about the infrastructure of the sanatorium, specialists, services, view your schedule and independently sign up for additional procedures.
Maid
The maid saw the task of cleaning the room in the mobile and completed it before the arrival of a new guest. The accommodation service monitors the status of cleaning the premises and pleases customers with settling in without delay. The customer can evaluate the quality of cleaning in the mobile application.
0 / 0
First visit doctor
At the reception, the doctor corrects the treatment program. As a result, an individual schedule is formed taking into account the compatibility of procedures and the wishes of the patient.
No queues
The doctor received a notification about a new patient before the appointment and studied the medical history (if the patient came to the sanatorium again or was in another sanatorium of the network). Automation of the schedule and reception of patients eliminates queues in front of offices.

Notification of research results

All employees involved in the care of a patient should keep each other informed of important updates related to the ward. And adjust the program for the guest. This will allow us to develop an individual “formula” of treatment that will solve the client’s problems more effectively than others.
