Medicine

Doctors like to work in MIS “Sanatorium” — it is convenient and created taking into account the needs and standards of the country’s leading health resorts. We help medical staff to work in a high load mode and take on routine tasks. We free up time for the most important thing — treating patients.
Order a consultation

Electronic medical records (IB)

The main document in which everything that happens with the patient is recorded. The basic fields of the medical history (name, age, place of residence) enter the system at the placement stage. Each IB is created on the basis of electronic survey forms that are filled in by medical staff. The results of visits to each doctor of the sanatorium, the results of analyzes, studies, and procedures are loaded into the history. When the patient arrives again, information about him will be easy to find.

Print Minimum

Filling in is simplified as much as possible and practically does not require manual input: the doctor simply notes the appropriate option — if there is none, it is entered once and remembered by the system. The speech recognition system works.

Appointment scheduling

In just one click, the system creates a patient schedule for the entire period of treatment — all procedures are evenly distributed over the time spent in the sanatorium. Taking into account the compatibility of procedures and any rules (gender, age, floor, patient category, schedule, workload and vacation schedule for specialists).

Vacation procedures from the tablet

Effective accounting of all released procedures, excluding abuse. To identify a patient, it is enough to attach a guest card to the tablet, and the dismissing specialist will immediately see what procedure is prescribed, what materials to use, what zones of influence. After the service is rendered, it is enough to press the “release” button, and the standard amount of materials (therapeutic mud, preparations, consumables) will be written off from the warehouse balance.

Read more

Interpreting Patient Scores

The expert module “Sanatorium” interprets the results of research, calculates BMI, calories, and pays attention to indicators that go beyond the normative limits. The module will not replace a doctor, but will help with an intensive flow of patients.

Diagnosis Recommendations

After the diagnosis is made, the system will automatically display a list of recommended procedures. The list may include both generally accepted recommendations and institution standards. This will help the doctor recommend to the patient what is indicated for his condition. The function increases the effectiveness of treatment and the average revenue from additional services that are not included in the cost of the tour.

Read more

300 built-in protocols

The workspace of a doctor, from a therapist to a narrow specialist (nutritionist, cardiologist), is set up in accordance with the area of responsibility. That is why it is so convenient to work in the system — the doctor sees only the indicators he needs, if necessary, downloading data from other doctors. At the same time, any of the 300 ready-made protocols can be modified: remove or add buttons, menu items and areas for filling.

Motivation of medical staff

“Sanatorium” allows you to track and identify the most valuable employees. Additional indicators will make transparent the results of each employee and simplify the calculation of bonuses. Anything can be taken into account: the number of admitted patients, the proportion of repeat patients, the ratio of positive and negative reviews.

Individual power module

In many sanatoriums, food is part of the treatment. The diet prescribed by the doctor takes into account individual needs and limitations (allergies, lactose/gluten intolerance). This data is displayed on the desktop of the dietitian.

Doctor Events Module

The doctor receives notifications about scheduled appointments, new patients, and ready-made results from the laboratory. The results of the patient’s examinations and consultations of other doctors can be monitored in real time and the treatment plan can be quickly adjusted in accordance with new data. In such a workstation for a sanatorium, important information will never be lost.
